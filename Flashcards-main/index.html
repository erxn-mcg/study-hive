Flashcard Maker!








This is a pie chart showing (in blue) the proportion of students who use flashcards. As you can see, it is an overwhelming proportion, and this is for good reasons! - Flashcards engage your active recall meaning stronger neutron connections are formed in your brain. This makes it more likely you'll remember the information in the future. - Flashcards allow you to test yourself and this helps you figure out what information you can remember easily and what you need to work on. So, go ahead and make some flashcards, or have a look at the ready-made French, German, and Spanish vocab cards above :)
